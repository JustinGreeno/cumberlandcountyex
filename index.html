Welcome to the Fair!

For over a century, the Cumberland County Exhibition has brought together families, farmers, and fun-seekers from across Nova Scotia. Our annual agricultural fair celebrates the heart of rural life with livestock shows, 4-H competitions, homemade treats, and entertainment for all ages.

From the smell of fresh hay to the excitement of the midway, CCEx is where memories are made and traditions are kept alive. Join us this fall for another unforgettable celebration of our agricultural heritage!

Livestock Shows

Cattle, horses, sheep, goats, poultry and more! Watch our farmers showcase their finest animals.

4-H Competitions

Young farmers demonstrate their skills in showmanship, judging, and agricultural knowledge.

Midway & Rides

Thrilling rides, carnival games, and family fun for visitors of all ages!

A Proud Tradition

The Cumberland County Exhibition has been a cornerstone of community life in Nova Scotia for generations. What started as a simple gathering of local farmers has grown into one of the province's most anticipated annual events.

Each year, thousands of visitors walk through our gates to experience the authentic charm of a traditional agricultural fair. Whether you're a lifelong resident or a first-time visitor, the CCEx welcomes you to be part of our story.
